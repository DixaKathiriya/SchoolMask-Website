How to Care for your SchoolMaskPack

We care about mask care! To clean our Crayola Reusable Cloth Face Mask Set between uses, simply put the masks inside the mesh laundry bag provided, toss it in the washing machine and go. Please ensure to store clean masks and worn masks separately as to avoid cross contamination.


Follow our care instructions for the best results:

  • Wash before use.
  • Machine wash gentle cycle and tumble dry low inside the mesh laundry bag provided.
  • Wash with like colors.
  • Wash after each use.
  • Do not dry clean.
  • Do not iron the ear straps.
  • Store clean and used masks separately to avoid cross contamination.
