How to Wear SchoolMaskPack Masks

Wearing our Crayola Reusable Cloth Face Masks masks is as easy as 1, 2, 3!

Here's how to wear:

  • Step 1: With clean hands, place a washed, clean mask over your face.
  • Step 2: Place the ear straps over your ears.
  • Step 3: Move the adjuster behind the ear straps to tighten or loosen the straps for your preferred secure fit.
  • Step 4: Press down on the wire nose clip to create a secure fit around the face.
  • Step 5: Place used masks in the provided mesh laundry bag after use to store and keep separate until washed.
